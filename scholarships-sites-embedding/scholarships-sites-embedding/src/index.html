For students attending Texas institutions of higher education

Can be used for tuition, fees, books, and other school-related expenses

No discrimination based on race, creed, religion, color, sex, or national origin

Awards at least $15,000 annually

George and Mary Josephine Hamman Foundation Scholarship

Awards scholarships to Houston area high school seniors for undergraduate study

Recipients can choose any major at any 4-year college or university in the U.S.

Applications open after September 15th of the senior year

Do not apply before taking the SAT or ACT

Minimum SAT score: 1000 (Math and Reading/Writing combined)

Minimum ACT score: 21

Scholarship amount $25,000
